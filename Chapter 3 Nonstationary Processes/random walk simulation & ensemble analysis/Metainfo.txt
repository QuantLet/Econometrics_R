################################################################################################Name of Quantlet: Econometrics_with_R 

Published in: Econometrics_R

Description: 
This R script simulates a single realization of a random walk process and generates multiple 
independent random walk paths to perform ensemble analysis. It estimates the sample mean and variance 
from the single realization, calculates the ensemble average and variance at a specific time point (t = 100) 
across 50 paths, and visualizes the results. The single path and ensemble random walk plots are saved 
as PNG files in the working directory.

Keywords: 
Random Walk, Stochastic Process, Simulation, Ensemble Average, Variance Estimation, Time Series, Visualization, R
