################################################################################################Name of Quantlet: Econometrics_with_R 

Published in: Econometrics_R

Description: 
This R script simulates a random walk process with a constant drift term and generates 
a corresponding time series plot. The script also performs the Augmented Dickey-Fuller (ADF) 
unit root test with drift, selecting the lag order based on the Akaike Information Criterion (AIC). 
Both the plot of the random walk and the ADF test results are produced, with the figure 
saved as a PNG file in the working directory.

Keywords: 
Random Walk, Drift, Simulation, Time Series, Unit Root Test, ADF Test, Stationarity, Visualization, R
