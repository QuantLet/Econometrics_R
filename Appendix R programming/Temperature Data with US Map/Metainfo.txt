Description:
This R script downloads and processes daily maximum temperature data (TMAX) from the Global Historical Climatology Network (GHCN) for all U.S. states over the period 2015â€“2024 using representative weather stations. It computes annual and 10-year average temperatures, merges the results with U.S. state shapefiles, and visualizes the spatial distribution of average temperatures across the United States using choropleth maps. The script also includes a separate plot showing the daily temperature profile for New York City in 2014.
