################################################################################################Name of Quantlet: Econometrics_with_R_mtcars_regression

Published in: Econometrics_R

Description: 
This R script sets the working directory to the location of the script itself,
simulates a single realization of a random walk process, generates 50 independent random walk paths, 
plots the single realization and the ensemble of paths, and computes sample-based and ensemble-based 
statistics including means and variances.

Keywords: 
Random Walk, Time Series, Stochastic Process, Simulation, Ensemble Average, Variance Estimation, R

Author: Jiajing Sun

Submitted: 29 April 2025
