################################################################################################Name of Quantlet: Econometrics_with_R_mtcars_regression

Published in: Econometrics_R

Description: 
This R script visualizes classical kernel functions (Truncated, Bartlett, Daniell, Parzen, and QS) 
and their corresponding Fourier transforms. For each kernel, the function itself and its Fourier transform 
are plotted side-by-side. All the combined plots are displayed and saved as a single PNG file. 
The script uses ggplot2 for visualization and gridExtra to arrange the plots neatly.

Keywords: 
Kernel Functions, Fourier Transforms, Spectral Analysis, Visualization, Time Series, ggplot2, R
